## Task: Presents the monthly count of successful orders {{period}} in MM:COUNT format
### Example 1 (108): Presents the monthly count of successful orders 01/2023-05/2023 in MM:COUNT format
Navigated to the REPORTS section to access order reporting functionality for monthly breakdown.
```click("339")```
Clicked the "Orders" report link under the Sales section to access order statistics.
```click("383")```
Changed the report period from "Day" to "Month" to prepare for generating monthly order counts.
```select_option("749", "Month")```
Set the date range from January 1, 2023 to May 31, 2023 in the filter form to analyze data for that time period.
```fill("757", "01/01/2023")```
Set the "To" date to 05/31/2023 to complete the date range for the report.
```fill("764", "05/31/2023")```
Clicked the "Show Report" button to generate the monthly order counts report for January through May 2023.
```click("714")```
Extracted monthly order data from the generated report and formatted it as MM:COUNT pairs.
```send_msg_to_user("Monthly count of successful orders 01/2023-05/2023:\n01:12\n02:7\n03:5\n04:10\n05:8")```
